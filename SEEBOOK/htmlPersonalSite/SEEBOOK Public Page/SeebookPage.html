Welcome to SEEBOOK! SEEBOOK is an online place where students from different schools can communicate and create virtual classrooms. As well as for non-school related purposes. Here are some rules you must follow:

  1. There is no tolerance for bullying or making fun of others, for all servers will be monitored.
  2. Make sure to leave friendly suggestions or ideas that could be used to make the SEEBOOK enviornment better!
  3. Have each other's back, don't be afraid to ask for help!

More Information:

Before you get enrolled in SEEBOOK, you need to know the different sections and parts of SEEBOOK. It has 4 different parts...

  • SEEBOOK Public: As you can guess, SEEBOOK Public have servers where everyone who logs in can see what you typed.
  • SEEBOOK Private: Now unlike SEEBOOK Public, private server messages can only be seen by the people invited into that server, which means not everyone can see it. That doesn't mean it's still not monitored by our staff.
  • SEEBOOK Post: SEEBOOK Post on the other hand, can be private or public. It's used for quick posts or notices.
  • SEEBOOK Clubs: The club part of SEEBOOK is where you can join or create your own club. Such as, the book club. They have their own terms and rules within their club, but if a rule disobeys SEEBOOK, the club will be shut down immediately.

Getting Started:

Remember that, as you will type in your username and password below, and be teleported to a selection of SEEBOOK Public, Private, Post, or Clubs.
